The Living Legacy

The seed planted in Bungoma has grown branches across East Africa, creating a dynasty of entrepreneurs and community leaders.

Daughter, visionary entrepreneur, and one of East Africa's most successful businesswomen. From Oasis Mall in Kampala to the Atiak Sugar Factory, she transformed her mother's lessons into an empire that uplifts thousands.

"I am my mother's daughter. Every brick I've laid stands on the foundation she built."

New Nyanza: The Institution

More than a business, New Nyanza Wholesalers Ltd is a living monument to discipline, continuity, and matriarchal leadership.

The Heart of Bungoma Commerce

Founded by Sarah Hersi Ali, New Nyanza has been a cornerstone of Bungoma's economy for decades. What began as a small trading operation evolved into a wholesale institution supplying essential goods throughout Western Kenya.

Mama Hersi's philosophy of "zero waste, maximum value" transformed New Nyanza into more than a business—it became a community hub where relationships were as important as transactions.

Today, New Nyanza stands as a testament to her enduring presence. Despite health challenges and changing economies, she remains at the helm, demonstrating that true leadership never retires.
